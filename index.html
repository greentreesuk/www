About

We all struggle with difficult feelings at times in our lives. I am an approachable clinical psychologist who can help you if you are feeling anxious, stressed, depressed or low in mood. We can think together about your individual needs in a confidential and supportive meeting. I can help you to make sense of your emotions and think about the next steps to work towards your goals.

My approach to therapy is that I draw upon a range of approaches including Compassion Focused Therapy, Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, Acceptance and Commitment Therapy and Family therapy ideas.

I am registered with the Health Care Professions Council and British Psychological Society.

Services

I provide a range of services including psychological help for:

  • Anxiety
  • Low mood
  • Stress management
  • Loss and bereavement
  • Sleep difficulties
  • Confidence
  • Family Issues
  • Anger
